Best Meats

Chicken

"Chicken meat contains about two to three times as much polyunsaturated fat as most types of red meat when measured as weight percentage"

Mutton

"Harvested from a fully-grown sheep, mutton is loved by a large number of people around the world. Mutton is vigorously rich in iron and zinc. Iron is primarily utilized in the body in the formation of blood cells and blood."

Fish

"Fish is filled with omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins such as D and B2 (riboflavin). Fish is packed with protein, vitamins, and nutrients that can lower blood pressure and help reduce the risk of a heart attack."
